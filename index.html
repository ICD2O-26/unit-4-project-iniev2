Automatons of the past and present

By Izzy!


What is an automaton?

An automaton is a relatively self-operating/controlling mechanism designed to follow a sequence of predetermined instructions.


Where can my technology be used in the real world?

Older automatons (c. 1800s) were used for entertainment, moving throughout exhibits before retiring when they became too brittle. Very old automatons, however, were used in a more practical manner. The antikythera mechanism is an ancient device that could predict astronomical positions. It is the oldest known form of the analogue computer.

Aside from old ways of use, automatons are still used in modern day. The coocoo clock is an automaton, as well as the well-known toy of the monkey bashing the cymbals together (and any other self-moving wind up toy).
